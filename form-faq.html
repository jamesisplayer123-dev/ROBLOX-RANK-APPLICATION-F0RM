üìã Applying

What is this application portal for?

This portal lets ROBLOX players apply for community ranks (Member, Contributor, Developer, Tester, Admin). Applications are reviewed by moderators and admins.

How do I apply?

Go to Apply Now, fill in your ROBLOX username, contact, favorite game, achievements, desired rank and why you deserve it, then submit. You'll receive a reference like RBXAPP1234567890.

What information is required?

  • ROBLOX Username and Display Name
  • Email and Discord contact
  • Favorite ROBLOX game
  • Account age,e
  • Desired rank and motivation statement

Which ROBLOX games are supported?

Common games listed include: Adopt Me!, Welcome to Bloxburg, Tower of Hell, Prison Life, ROBLOX High School, Arsenal, Murder Mystery 2, Bedwars. Pick the game where your achievements are strongest.

‚è±Ô∏è Status & Timelines

How long does review take?

Typical timeline: initial screening 1-3 days, detailed review 3-7 days, final decision within 7-14 days. Busy periods may extend this.

How do I check my application status?

Use your reference (RBXAPP...) at the Check Status page. Enter the code to see the current status and timeline.

üë• Moderators & Admins

How do moderators review applications?

Moderators use the Admin Panel (Admin) to filter, open, and evaluate applications. They can approve or reject and leave notes (mocked in this demo).

How do I get moderator/admin access?

Admins add team members via the User Management tab in the Admin Panel. Roles: Reviewer (view only), Moderator (approve/reject), Admin (manage users).
