â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many users can I add?

A: As many as you want! Add as many team members as needed to manage applications.

Q: Can I change someone's role later?

A: Yes! Go to User Management, click "Edit" on their card, and change the role anytime.

Q: What if I accidentally give someone admin access?

A: No problem! Edit their role and downgrade them to Moderator or Reviewer.

Q: Can users see each other?

A: Yes! All team members can see the Team Members list in User Management.

Q: What happens if I remove a user?

A: They lose access to the admin panel. They can still apply for ranks as regular users.

Q: In a real system, would this need authentication?

A: Yes! In production, you'd need login credentials, passwords, and permission verification. This demo gives everyone access for testing.

Q: Can I export the user list?

A: Currently you can view it in the Team Members section. Future versions could have export features.

Q: How do users know their passwords?

A: In a real system, you'd send them login links via email. For this demo, there are no passwords.
